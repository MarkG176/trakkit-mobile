# Seeding Agent Guide

Welcome to Trakkit! This guide walks you through your daily workflow as a seeding agent.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Log in** using the magic link sent to your email or phone. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
2. Once logged in, you'll land on the **Dashboard** — your home base for the day.

---

## Your Daily Workflow

### Step 1: Check In
- Tap the **Record Attendance** button on the Dashboard.
- Allow location access when prompted — this is required for check-in.
- Take a selfie when asked. Make sure your face is clearly visible and well-lit.
- If check-in fails, make sure you are **within range** of your assigned location and that your GPS is turned on.

### Step 2: Add Your Location
- Go to the **Routes** tab at the bottom of the screen.
- Use the **Add Location** form to register the area you're working in today.

### Step 3: Record Your Sales
- Go to **Inventory** to see the products assigned to you.
- When you make a sale, tap **Record Sale** and fill in the product, quantity, and customer details.
- Sales are tracked throughout the day and shown in your evening report.

### Step 4: Give Away Products
- If you're distributing free samples, go to **Inventory** and use the **Give Products** option.
- Record who received the items and how many were given.

### Step 5: Check Out
- When your day is done, go back to the **Dashboard** and tap the attendance button to **Check Out**.
- An **Evening Report** will pop up automatically. This shows:
  - A summary of your sales for the day
  - A space to write any notes (challenges, feedback, observations)
  - An option to upload **Engagement Photos** from the field
- Review everything, then tap **Submit Report**.

## Tips & Troubleshooting

### "Check-in failed" or "Out of range"
- Make sure your phone's **GPS/Location** is turned on (not just Wi-Fi location).
- Step outside if you're in a building — GPS signal can be weak indoors.
- Close and reopen the app, then try again.

### Selfie not uploading
- Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data).
- Make sure you've given the app **camera permissions** in your phone settings.
- Try switching between your front and back camera, then switch back.

### Sales or giveaways not showing up
- Pull down to **refresh** the page.
- Make sure you selected the correct **workspace** at the top of the screen.
- If the issue persists, check out and check back in.

### App feels slow or stuck
- Close the app completely and reopen it.
- Clear your browser cache if you're using the web version.
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection.

### Evening Report didn't appear
- The report only appears when you **check out**. Make sure you tapped the checkout button.
- If it still doesn't show, go to your **Profile** page to review your daily summary.

### Photos not uploading in Evening Report
- Large photos take longer. Wait for the progress bar to finish.
- If it fails, try uploading fewer photos at a time.
- Ensure you have a strong internet connection.
